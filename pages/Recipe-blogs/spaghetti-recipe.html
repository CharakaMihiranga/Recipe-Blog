Classic Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 12 ounces
Pancetta or bacon, diced 4 ounces
Eggs 2 large
Grated Parmesan cheese 1 cup
Black pepper To taste
Salt To taste
Italian parsley, chopped (optional) For garnish

Instructions

1. Cook Spaghetti:

Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Reserve about 1/2 cup of pasta cooking water before draining the spaghetti.

2. Cook Pancetta or Bacon:

In a large skillet, cook the diced pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy and golden brown. Remove from the skillet and drain on paper towels.

3. Prepare Carbonara Sauce:

In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper until well combined.

4. Combine:

In the same skillet used to cook the pancetta or bacon, add the cooked spaghetti and cooked pancetta or bacon. Toss to combine. Remove the skillet from the heat and quickly pour in the carbonara sauce, tossing continuously to coat the spaghetti evenly. If the sauce is too thick, add a splash of reserved pasta cooking water to loosen it up.

5. Serve:

Divide the spaghetti carbonara among plates. Garnish with chopped Italian parsley if desired. Serve immediately, and enjoy this classic and comforting Italian dish!
