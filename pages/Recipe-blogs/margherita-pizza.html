Homemade Margherita Pizza

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Pizza dough 1 pound (homemade or store-bought)
Tomatoes, thinly sliced 2 medium
Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced 8 ounces
Fresh basil leaves A handful
Extra virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic cloves, minced 2
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Instructions

1. Preheat Oven:

Preheat your oven to the highest temperature setting, usually around 475째F to 500째F (245째C to 260째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

2. Roll Out Dough:

On a lightly floured surface, roll out the pizza dough into a circle or rectangle, about 1/4 inch thick. Transfer the rolled-out dough onto a lightly floured pizza peel or a parchment-lined baking sheet.

3. Prepare Toppings:

In a small bowl, mix together the minced garlic and extra virgin olive oil. Brush the garlic-infused oil over the surface of the pizza dough. Arrange the thinly sliced tomatoes and fresh mozzarella cheese slices evenly over the dough. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

4. Bake:

Carefully transfer the assembled pizza onto the preheated pizza stone in the oven, or place the baking sheet directly on the oven rack. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

5. Garnish:

Remove the pizza from the oven and immediately garnish with fresh basil leaves. Drizzle with a little more extra virgin olive oil if desired.

6. Serve:

Slice the homemade Margherita pizza and serve hot. Enjoy the delicious flavors of this classic Italian pizza!
