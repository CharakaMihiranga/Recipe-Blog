Delicious Meatballs Recipe

Ingredients

For the Meatballs:

Ingredient Quantity
Ground beef 500 grams
Breadcrumbs 1/2 cup
Onion, finely chopped 1
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Egg 1
Parsley, chopped 2 tablespoons
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

For the Sauce:

Ingredient Quantity
Tomato sauce 2 cups
Crushed tomatoes 1 can (400g)
Onion, diced 1
Garlic, minced 3 cloves
Dried oregano 1 teaspoon
Dried basil 1 teaspoon
Red pepper flakes 1/2 teaspoon
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Instructions

1. Prepare the Meatballs:

In a large bowl, combine ground beef, breadcrumbs, chopped onion, minced garlic, egg, chopped parsley, salt, and black pepper. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly incorporated. Form the mixture into small meatballs.

2. Brown the Meatballs:

Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Brown the meatballs on all sides until they are cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.

3. Prepare the Sauce:

In the same skillet, saut√© diced onion and minced garlic until they are soft and translucent. Add tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, dried oregano, dried basil, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper. Stir well and let the sauce simmer for about 10 minutes.

4. Simmer the Meatballs in Sauce:

Return the browned meatballs to the skillet with the sauce. Coat the meatballs evenly with the sauce. Cover and let them simmer in the sauce for another 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.

5. Serve:

Once the meatballs are cooked through and the sauce has thickened, remove from heat. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. These delicious meatballs can be served over pasta or with crusty bread for dipping.
