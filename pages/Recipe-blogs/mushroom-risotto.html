Creamy Mushroom Risotto Recipe

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity
Arborio rice 1 1/2 cups
Butter 4 tablespoons
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Onion, finely chopped 1
Garlic, minced 2 cloves
Button mushrooms, sliced 8 ounces
Vegetable or chicken broth 4 cups
Dry white wine 1/2 cup
Grated Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup
Heavy cream 1/4 cup
Fresh parsley, chopped 2 tablespoons
Salt To taste
Black pepper To taste

Instructions

1. Sauté Mushrooms:

In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and tender. Remove the mushrooms from the skillet and set aside.

2. Cook Onion and Garlic:

In the same skillet, add the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and olive oil. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.

3. Toast Rice:

Add the Arborio rice to the skillet with the onion and garlic. Cook, stirring constantly, for about 2 minutes until the rice is lightly toasted.

4. Add Wine:

Pour the white wine into the skillet and cook, stirring constantly, until the wine is absorbed by the rice.

5. Cook Risotto:

Gradually add the broth, about 1/2 cup at a time, stirring frequently. Allow each addition of broth to be absorbed before adding more. Continue cooking until the rice is creamy and tender, but still slightly firm to the bite, about 20-25 minutes.

6. Finish with Cream and Cheese:

Once the rice is cooked, stir in the sautéed mushrooms, grated Parmesan cheese, and heavy cream. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until heated through and creamy.

7. Serve:

Garnish the creamy mushroom risotto with chopped fresh parsley before serving. Enjoy this delicious and comforting dish as a main course or as a side!
